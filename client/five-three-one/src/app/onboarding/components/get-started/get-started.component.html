PROGRESS: 5/3/1

Simple, Progressive Weightlifting.

How It Works

Train 3-4 days a week

Workouts are programmed around: Squat, Bench, Deadlift, and Shoulder Press

Each week you will lift a higher percentage of your one-rep max (1RM)

After 16 workouts, you will add weight to your 1RM and start the percentage cycles over.

Break personal records and get stronger.
